About Us

Who are we?


We are Undergraduate students of Sabaragamuwa University of sri lanka, Department of Computing and Information Systems. We developed this website as a project and this is a online platform for all internet users to predict their illnesses by providing their symptoms. If you are interested or find this is valuable or not please feel free to share your feedback with us



What are we doing here?


We all affect with different types of illnesses and sometimes we don't know what are our illnesses or how serious they are. With this website, we predict your illnesses and provide details about your illnesses. Then you will be able to understand how serious your illnesses



Why we are different?


There may be some platform which provide you facility to predict your illnesses from your symptoms. And also when you go for a doctor, that doctor may be not a specialist on your illness. With our online website we provide you specialist doctors who are well experinced on your illnesses and their channeling and contact information



Who are our doctors?

Currently this website is able to predict over 40+ illnesses/diseases by their symptoms and we provide/suggest you specialist doctors who are well experienced on those 40+ illnesses and diseases. And also we provide you Doctor's Contact and channeling information
